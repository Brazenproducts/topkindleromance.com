Can I read Kindle books on my phone or tablet?
Yes! The free Kindle app is available for iOS and Android. You don't need a Kindle device to read Kindle books or access Kindle Unlimited — just download the app.
What's the difference between romantasy and paranormal romance?
Romantasy is fantasy with romance as a central story element — the fantasy world and magic system are integral to the plot. Paranormal romance typically has a contemporary setting with supernatural beings (vampires, werewolves, etc.). Fae romance sits squarely in the romantasy category.
